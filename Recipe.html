Recipe: Leche Flan

Ingredients

  • 10 pieces egg
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1 cup fresh milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Methods:

  1. Using all egss, seperate the yolk from the egg white.
  2. Place the egg yolks in a big bowl then beat using fork.
  3. Add the condensed milk and mix.
  4. Pour in the fresh milk and vanilla, mix well.
  5. Put the mold (llanera) on top of the stove and heat using low fire.
  6. Put-in the granulatedd sugar on th mold and mix throughly until it melt and spread on the flat side of the mold.
  7. Wait for 5 minutes then pour the egg yolk and milk texture on the mold.
  8. Cover the top of the mold using an aluminum foil and steam for 35 minutes.
  9. After steamiing, let it cool on refrigerator.
  10. Serve for Dessert and Enjoy!
